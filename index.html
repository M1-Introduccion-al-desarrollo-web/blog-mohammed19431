ABOUT GHANA:

Ghana, located in West Africa along the Gulf of Guinea, is known for its rich history and cultural heritage.
It was the first sub-Saharan African country to gain independence from colonial rule in 1957.
The official language is English, and its economy thrives on exports like gold, cocoa, and oil.
Ghana is also celebrated for its vibrant traditions, lush, landscapes, and historic sites like Cape Coast Castle
